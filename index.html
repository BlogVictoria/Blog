Big-Ben

I visit Big-Ben a lot. My house is only about 40 minutes away from it. Every year millions of people visit this amazing place which is located in London. The first time I visited it was my first Easter in London. I really loved it and I would really recomend you to visit it.

Hapton Court

I went to see Hapton Court twice. It was once a home to one of the first Tudor rulers - Henry the 8th, and his relatives. Now it is a musiem which shows what was life like during the Tudor times. Henry was a very wealthy person who had 2 chocolate factories, a giant master bedroom and a enormous dining hall. Most of the things now are replicas, but some are real.
